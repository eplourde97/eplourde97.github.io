Rocket League Clips

Rocket League is one of my favorite video games to play when I have some down time. It's basically just soccer being played with cars that can fly. There are other game modes that you can play, though, as you will see in the last two clips. I play competitive 2v2 most of the time, which means I rank up or derank based on if I win or lose. It's the same sort of ranking system as League of Legends if you have ever played that. I score most of my nice looking goals in competitive mode, but I'm usually far too pumped after the match to remember to save the clip. All of these clips are from when I'm just kicking around on casual gameplay. Please enjoy!
